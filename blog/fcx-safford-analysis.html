Deep Dive: Freeport-McMoRan's Impact in Safford, AZ

Published: March 29, 2025

Freeport-McMoRan (FCX) is a dominant force in Arizona's Copper Corridor, and its Safford operations are a cornerstone of the regional economy. This analysis explores FCX's activities in Safford, its local economic contributions, and the broader investment context for the company.

Freeport-McMoRan Safford Operations Overview

  • Location: Graham County, southeastern Arizona (~52,000 acres).
  • Methodology: Open-pit copper mining using solvent extraction-electrowinning (SX-EW).
  • Production: Approximately 200 million pounds of copper cathode annually (based on 2022 data).
  • Reserves: Estimated 1.4 billion pounds recoverable copper, supporting operations beyond 2030.
  • Technology: Utilizes automation like autonomous haul trucks and focuses on water conservation.
